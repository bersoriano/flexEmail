We are excited to announce our Flex Apartment Listings site for Charlotte, North Carolina, allowing you to browse properties in your area that support Flex! Our listings site makes it easier for you to find your next home, where you can benefit from a more flexible way to pay your rent. When you find an apartment that you love, submit your information on the web form directly on the listing and a representative will be in touch to provide you with more information.

Today, we are beginning our limited rollout to select cities, including Austin and San Antonio, Texas, as well as Charlotte, North Carolina. However, we would love to expand out to new regions as soon as we can!
